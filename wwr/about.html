Dee Water Adventure Company

Our mission is to provide safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable water adventure experiences that connect people with nature and create lifelong memories for adventurers of all skill levels.

History

Founded in 1998 by river enthusiast Nkosana Dlamini, Dee Water adventure Co. began with a single raft and a passion for adventure. What started as weekend trips for friends has grown into a premier rafting company serving thousands of adventurers each year. Our commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and unforgettable experiences has remained unchanged since day one. Over the years, we've expanded our operations to include three major rivers and have trained over 50 certified guides who share our love for the river.
